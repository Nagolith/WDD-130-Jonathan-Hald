Overview

Purpose

The purpose of my portfolio website is to show my works and passions in a visually pleasing way to employers to help introduce my works and help them get a feel for who I am personally more so then they could from just a resume could ever say alone.

Audience

The target audience is directly employers that are wanting to see if I might be a good fit for their company. They want to know more about a person than a resume could ever represent and therefore they want to pick developers that they think would be qualified and would be a good fit into the company to interview. They want to waste as little time and resources on looking for potential developers as possible.

Content

Home page

The site will offer a consolidated menu to navigate to my projects and to learn more about me. They will be able to navigate to easily to my works through links to my GitHub projects. The images will be links. There will also be detailed descriptions of my projects for each of them and how they work. There will also be a page of information on my personal life the visitors can read to learn more about me as a person.

Images for the Home page

About Me

Expectations and About Me: They may wonder more about who I am and what I have done. This site will give a much better picture of who I am and what I have done. It will possibly answer some questions that employers might have before reaching out for an interview. It would help them see me through more than just a resume. It will have information about my life, my likes, values, family, aspirations, and my work ethic.

Project and Works

They will want to come to my website because it will be an intuitive and visually pleasing site for learning more about myself and seeing my portfolio. It will make it much simpler for an employer to get a good idea of who I am and my works. This website will reduce the amount of work an employer would need to do to navigate through information about myself. They will be presented will the desired information and have access to all the projects that I have available. The images will be links to my projects and when hovered over there will be a short animation and display a description of the project.
